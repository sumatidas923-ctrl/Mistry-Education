Why Only Math and Geography?

Many tutoring centers attempt to teach every subject, often spreading their tutors too thin. At Mistry Education, we believe in narrow specialization. We focus entirely on two core intellectual paths for **Grades 8 to 12**:

"Mathematics develops rigorous logical reasoning, patterns identification, and numeric problem-solving. Geography instills dynamic global awareness, physical earth understanding, and spatial analysis."

By offering deep expert tutoring in only these areas for high-schoolers and middle-schoolers, we can promise up-to-date curriculum outlines, high-quality test prep materials (like AP, SAT), and expert instructors who actually hold degrees in their respective fields.

Our Core Teaching Tenets

1. Diagnostic-First Strategy

We analyze prior grade reports and run a customized diagnostics test to target exact subject gaps before tutoring begins.

2. Grades 8–12 Academic Focus

Our resources are engineered specifically to help students transition from early Algebra to advanced AP/IB Calculus and Geography exams.

3. Direct Active Communication

Our parents receive structured summary notes after every tutoring session, detailing homework progress and quiz scores.
